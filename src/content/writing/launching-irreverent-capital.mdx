> _“If the culture is heavily infused with respect and worship of ancient wisdom so that any intellectual innovation is considered deviant and blasphemous, technological creativity will be similarly constrained. **Irreverence is a key to progress**” -_ Joel Mokyr, _A Culture of Growth_

**What is Irreverent and Why I’m Starting It**

Irreverent is a fund that builds important businesses, similar to ARCH Venture Partners, Shaper Capital, and 8VC Build. We are currently working on several theses and will start by building one company in 2025, likely with one great partner.

This model is the fund of the future, and I find it inspiring for reasons that follow below. I will start many companies with the venture creation model over the next 50 years and Irreverent will be a proper vehicle to do so. 

**Vision**

In 2024, The United States faces many challenges, including a lagging military-industrial complex and rising geopolitical conflict, difficulty finding skilled labor, and the continued lack of understanding of the human condition. 

On a more optimistic note, technological advancements today enable us to solve more problems than ever before. New solutions can be created daily and much of the world will look incredibly different 5, 10, and 50 years from now as the impact of technology grows exponentially.

**The Bottleneck to Progress is Innovation**

The main lever for progress in an industrial society is technical innovation. However, many ambitious people are working on problems that don’t move the needle for the next generation or are working within likely, structured systems that don’t innovate.

The future must be pioneered by everyday citizens individually choosing to solve important problems. Richard Hamming of Bell Labs asks us, 

> _"What's the most important problem in your field right now? How come you're not working on it?"_ 

**A Cultural Solution**

Startups have taught me that culture is critical. Values such as irreverence, curiosity, agency, and industriousness are necessary to challenge the status quo. While the demand for new solutions remains high, the supply of innovators and courage is relatively low. Irreverent will build upon this inefficiency.

After joining Shaper Capital at the ground floor, I learned great lessons from the incredibly smart and nice people that I worked with. It was the best job I’ve ever had, and I left a better person and professional. Many of these lessons are also documented in the [Medium Essays](https://travismay.medium.com/) of Travis May.

My experiences over the past year have shaped my view on [velocity](https://substack.com/home/post/p-139030381), [culture](https://jonathanpolitzki.substack.com/p/culture), impact, venture creation, and the future that should exist.

**What Irreverent Will Do**

Irreverent is a venture creation firm that will launch one company per year. Launched portfolio companies will lean into cultures of execution and focus. We will tackle industry-agnostic problems using AI and Middleware that:

(1) we are well-positioned to solve

(2) have strong business economics 

(3) are important for society

(4) are not popular and largely ignored 

**Problems Our Model Will Solve**

1.  **Focus:** Human focus is the most misallocated resource on Earth. The bottleneck of progress is hard work, agency, and great engineering. 
    
2.  **VC Model:** The venture capital model is broken. VC is oversaturated, incrementalist / lacks conviction, and investing has continued trending downstream. The best way to win the deal is to create it.
    
3.  **Broken Risk Calculus:** Many people miscalculate the risk of entrepreneurship. Impact is an equally important consideration.
    
4.  **Structure**: Many large companies impose too much structure, which hampers the ability to create and the freedom for individual growth. Both of which are crucial to new companies.
    
5.  **Equity**: Humans are meant to have heavy equity in solving important problems they are all-in on.
    

**Irreverent Capital Structure**

Irreverent will have three arms. We are high-conviction and are comfortable making big bets. 

We are not a VC. We build important companies and often partner with a great entrepreneur, academic, or company. We focus on maintaining lean, high returns and expand at the rate we can maintain density of culture. We will not take outside capital until the model is proven. 

“Reflexivity”

Money runs the world, technology changes it, and we use both. Our companies reinforce our fund and our fund reinforces our companies. We reinvest our winnings.

1.  The base layer and first arm of Irreverent will be a liquid capital base meant to be the soil in which we spring new companies. This will be the least important arm of the company.
    

2.  The second arm is a venture investment arm that makes rare, insider, opportunistic investments in early-stage companies in which we have high conviction. 
    

3.  The venture creation arm will be the cornerstone of our fund. We will operate lean. We will consistently build strong companies.
